Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the Smart Incident Indicator platform.

We aggregate data from multiple maritime intelligence sources including the IMB Piracy Reporting Centre, national meteorological departments, port authorities worldwide, NATO maritime advisories, and coastal surveillance systems. All data is verified and cross-referenced before being displayed on the platform.
The risk score (0-100) is calculated using a multi-factor algorithm that considers: incident severity, proximity to the shipment route, number of affected ports on the route, historical incident frequency in the region, current weather conditions, and geopolitical stability of transit zones.
Yes! The platform provides a RESTful API that can be integrated with any existing logistics, ERP, or supply chain management system. We support JSON data exchange and webhook notifications for real-time event streaming.
The platform is fully responsive and works across all devices — desktop, tablet, and mobile. The dashboard automatically adapts its layout for optimal viewing on any screen size.
Incident data is updated in near real-time with an average latency of under 30 seconds from source detection to dashboard display. Port status and weather data refresh every 5 minutes, while shipment tracking updates every 15 minutes.
